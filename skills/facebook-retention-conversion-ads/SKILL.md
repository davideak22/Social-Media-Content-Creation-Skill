# Facebook Retention & Conversion Ads (Social Proof Method)

A guide for setting up and scaling high-converting Facebook and Meta Ads by amplifying high-performing organic short-form videos. This newbie-friendly method uses existing social proof (likes, comments, shares) to maximize conversion and retention rates at a low starting budget.

---

## Why This Method Works

- **Preserves Social Proof:** By using the **Use Existing Post** feature in Meta, all existing organic likes, comments, and engagement are carried over to the ad, instantly building trust with cold audiences.
- **Low-Risk Testing:** Starts with a low daily budget ($5 to $10) spread across multiple video assets to identify outliers before scaling.
- **Organic Synergy:** Relies on finding high-performing organic content as the creative foundation, removing the guesswork of ad creative design.

---

## Step-by-Step Setup Guide

### Step 1: Identify High-Performing Organic Posts
Go to your professional dashboard on Instagram/Facebook and locate your best-performing posts.
- Look for posts with the highest **saves**, **leads**, or **comments**.
- Select **5 to 10** of these top-performing video assets to start testing.

### Step 2: Set Up the Meta Campaign
1. Open the **Meta Ads Manager** dashboard.
2. Click the green **+ Create** button.
3. Select **Leads** (or **Sales/Conversions** depending on your business goal) as the campaign objective and click **Continue**.
4. At the **Campaign Level**, choose your bid/budget strategy or leave it default.

### Step 3: Configure the Ad Set
1. Move to the **Ad Set Level**.
2. Select your conversion location (e.g., **Website**).
3. Under **Data Set** (Pixel), select your active **Meta Pixel**.
4. Set the **Daily Budget** to **$5 to $10**. Keep this budget low for the testing phase.
5. Set your targeting (keep it relatively broad or target your ICP).

### Step 4: Configure the Ad & Import Social Proof
1. Move to the **Ad Level**.
2. Under **Identity**, select your correct Facebook Page and Instagram Account.
3. Under **Ad Setup**, click the dropdown and select **Use Existing Post**.
4. Under **Ad Creative**, click **Select Post**.
5. Switch to the **Instagram** tab, browse to your chosen post, or input the specific **Short's ID** (copied from the Instagram URL structure).
6. Click **Continue**.
7. Under **Destination**, select **Website URL** and enter the destination link (e.g., calendar booking link or landing page).
8. Change the **Call to Action (CTA)** to your goal (e.g., **Book Now** or **Sign Up**).
9. Turn off **Advantage+ Creative** (to prevent Meta from altering your organic format).
10. Review your settings and click **Publish**.

### Step 5: Duplicate and Test
1. **Duplicate** the original ad set or ad.
2. Repeat **Step 4** to create ads for the other 5 to 10 selected videos within their own ad sets (budgeting $5-$10 per ad set).
3. Let the campaign run for a few days to collect initial data.

## Testing & Scaling Framework

| Day | Action | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Days 1–3** | Monitor lead volume, CTR, and CPC. | Establish baseline performance for each video. |
| **Days 4–7** | Identify the **outlier** (the clear best-performing ad). | Scale budget on the outlier by 10%–20% daily (max once per day). |
| **Week 2+** | Turn off underperforming ads. | Allocate budget to outliers and test new organic posts. |

---

## Best Practices for Long-Term Success

- **Continuous Hunting:** Frequently post new organic content and look for new winners. Ads naturally experience ad fatigue over time.
- **Budget Adjustments:** Only adjust budgets **once per day** to avoid resetting the Meta learning phase.
- **Keep it Simple:** This is the easiest, most newbie-friendly way to scale leads without complex funnel structures.
