# Account Growth by Follower Tier

A framework for tailoring your social media organic content strategy (especially for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts) based on your current follower count. This guide helps you focus on the right actions at each growth stage—from initial momentum to thought leadership.

---

## The Growth Strategy Framework

### 1. The Initial Phase (100 Followers)
- **Focus:** Build consistency, momentum, and persist through low engagement.
- **Reality:** View counts will likely be low. This is a normal and necessary humbling stage for new accounts.
- **Action:**
  - Keep pushing and posting regularly (preferably daily).
  - Expect that a single breakthrough video can launch your account's momentum.
  - Do not give up when view counts are low.

### 2. Consistency & Niche Selection (1,000 Followers)
- **Focus:** Niche alignment and hook/topic research.
- **Reality:** You have survived the hardest start-up phase. Views typically range from a few hundred to a few thousand.
- **Action:**
  - Stay highly consistent on your chosen topic—avoid jumping around topics.
  - Actively research virally proven hooks and topics within your niche and iterate on them.

### 3. Outlier Scaling (10k Followers)
- **Focus:** Identify and multiply the success of viral outlier videos.
- **Reality:** You have been posting for months to a year and have a few high-performing outlier videos.
- **Action:**
  - Double down on successful outlier videos using these three methods:
    1. **Format Rotation:** Keep the hook and value identical, but change the filming format to reach new audiences.
    2. **Value Adjustment:** Keep the format the same, but tweak the value slightly to deepen engagement with your current followers.
    3. **Hook/Topic Carryover:** Copy the hook, topic, or specific value into a completely new video structure.
  - Maintain a consistent posting schedule (preferably every single day).

### 4. Niche Authority (50k - 100k Followers)
- **Focus:** Personal brand integration and community trust.
- **Reality:** You have posted for a year or more. The audience follows you for your personality and voice, not just the raw information.
- **Action:**
  - Continue doubling down on successful topics using the three outlier scaling methods.
  - Research and iterate on viral hooks that align with your top-performing topics.
  - Begin introducing completely original ideas that do not rely on existing trends. Trust your intuition of what works.

### 5. Thought Leadership (1M+ Followers)
- **Focus:** Original thought leadership and authority.
- **Reality:** You are a top authority in your niche with deep audience trust and brand recognition.
- **Action:**
  - Focus primarily on original ideas and analyzing what works on your own account.
  - You no longer need to rely heavily on viral hook research or external trend-jacking to achieve high reach.

---

## Testing & Iteration Guidelines

- **Analyze Your Account first:** At higher follower tiers, your own account's performance history is the best research tool.
- **Iterate Proven Formats:** When you find an organic outlier, prioritize reproducing it in different formats before moving to new topics.
- **Optimize for Leads:** For businesses looking to grow and get leads, always use a clear Call to Action (CTA) matching your tier (e.g., prompting viewers to comment a specific keyword for more information).
