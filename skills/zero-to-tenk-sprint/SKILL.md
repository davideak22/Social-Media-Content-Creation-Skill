# Zero-to-10k Growth Sprint

A framework for executing a high-velocity, 30-day organic growth campaign to scale a brand or account from zero to 10,000 followers on short-form platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts).

---

## The 4-Step Growth Blueprint

When executing this sprint, follow these four chronological phases:

1. **Competitor Auditing & Script Creation:** Analyze virally proven formats and draft 30 niche-specific scripts.
2. **Batch Filming:** Capture all video assets in a single, high-efficiency 1–2 hour session.
3. **Engagement Editing & Multi-Platform Publishing:** Post-process videos for high visual retention and publish across all short-form platforms.
4. **App Engagement & Algorithmic Interaction:** Actively engage with the platforms to boost distribution signals.

---

## Step 1: Competitor Auditing & Script Creation

Instead of guessing what content will work, research what is already performing in the niche to draft 30 targeted scripts.

### Research Strategy
1. **Identify Top Creators:** Find the top 5 creators in the target niche.
2. **Audit Outliers:** Use tools like `viralfindr.com` to identify each creator's top 10 most viral posts.
3. **Analyze Viral Variables:**
   - **Hooks:** What visual or verbal pattern interrupts scroll?
   - **Topics:** What core subjects have the highest engagement?
   - **Video Length:** What is the average duration of the outliers?
   - **Structures & CTAs:** How is the value structured, and what action is requested?
4. **Keyword & Question Mining:** Search `quora.com` and Google to find the most frequently asked questions in the industry.

### Drafting 30 Scripts
Use the gathered insights to write 30 scripts (one for each day of the month). Combine these insights with templates from [short-form-content-structures](file:///Users/deakdavid/Documents/Portfolio/Social-Media-Content-Creation-Skill/skills/short-form-content-structures/SKILL.md).

---

## Step 2: Batch Filming

To maintain daily posting consistency, film the entire month's content in a single session.

### Batch Filming Guidelines
- **Time Commitment:** A month's worth of content (30 short videos) should take only 1–2 hours to film.
- **Preparation:** Have all 30 scripts ready, organized by clothing/background shifts if necessary to create visual variety.
- **Execution:** 
  - Keep the energy high and tone conversational.
  - Record multiple takes of the hook (0–3s) to ensure high click-through potential.
  - Deliver content with natural body language.

---

## Step 3: Engagement Editing & Multi-Platform Publishing

Maximize reach by optimizing editing styles and distributing to multiple platforms.

### Editing for Engagement
- **Fast Pacing:** Cut out all pauses, sighs, and dead air.
- **Visual Hooks:** Add dynamic text overlays and captions in the first 3 seconds.
- **Pacing Resets:** Use B-roll, green screen backgrounds, zoom-ins, or sound effects every 3–4 seconds.

### Multi-Platform Publishing
- Upload each video to **TikTok**, **Instagram Reels**, and **YouTube Shorts**.
- **SEO & Metadata:** Craft optimized titles, keyword-rich descriptions, and 3–5 relevant hashtags per post.
- **Bio Link:** Ensure the profile contains a single, clear call to action link (e.g., to a newsletter, landing page, or product).

---

## Step 4: Platform Interaction Flywheel

The algorithm favors active platform participants. Use engagement to signal the algorithm that the account is authentic.

### Algorithmic Warm-up & Interaction
- **Daily App Activity:** Spend 10–15 minutes daily interacting with other creators in the same niche.
- **Community Engagement:** Like and reply to every single comment on your own videos within the first hour of posting.
- **Engagement Loop:** Answer questions in the comments with video replies to double-down on high-performing topics.

---

## Zero-to-10k Sprint Checklist

Use this checklist to execute the growth sprint for the user:

- `[ ]` Step 1: Identify 5 top competitor accounts.
- `[ ]` Step 2: Extract top 10 viral posts per competitor.
- `[ ]` Step 3: Run Google & Quora search for high-intent questions.
- `[ ]` Step 4: Write 30 custom scripts using the analyzed structures.
- `[ ]` Step 5: Conduct the batch-filming session (1–2 hours).
- `[ ]` Step 6: Post-process & edit videos with engagement-reset cues.
- `[ ]` Step 7: Distribute daily to TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
- `[ ]` Step 8: Complete daily 15-minute interaction loops.
